Welcome to the Matching Pairs Game!



How to Play:

Your goal is to match the items from the right column with their matching item pair in the left column.

You need to click and drag an item from the right column and drop it into the correct row in the middle column.

To drop an item you must release your finger from the mouse button.

If the dropped item is a correct match, the dropped item will turn green.

If it is an incorrect match, the dropped item will return to its original location in the right column.

Once all the pairs have been matched and turned green, the game is won!.
